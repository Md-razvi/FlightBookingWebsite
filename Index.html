FAQ

How do I book a flight on SkyWings?

Booking a flight with SkyWings is easy! Simply visit our website at www.skywings.com and enter your departure and destination cities, travel dates, and the number of passengers. Our system will then display a range of flight options for you to choose from. Select your preferred flights, enter passenger details, and proceed to payment to confirm your booking.

Can I make changes to my booking after it's confirmed?

Yes, you can! We understand that plans can change. Depending on the airline's policies, you may be able to modify your booking. Log in to your SkyWings account, go to the "Manage Bookings" section, and follow the prompts to make changes. Please note that some changes may incur fees.
